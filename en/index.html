Frequently Asked Questions

What is Cluso Impostor?

Cluso Impostor is an offline social deduction party game for 3-20 players where you must find the impostor hiding among your group. It's similar to Among Us and Mafia but designed for in-person play with no internet required.

How many players do you need?

You can play with 3-20 players. The game works with small groups but is more exciting with 6 or more people. Only one device is needed for everyone.

Can you play without internet?

Yes! Cluso is completely offline. No WiFi or internet connection is required. Perfect for parties, camping, or anywhere without signal.

Do all players need the app?

No! Only one person needs to download the app. You pass one device around to all players - keeping the impostor's identity secret.

Is the game free?

Yes, Cluso is free to download and play. Available on iOS App Store.
